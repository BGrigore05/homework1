What is Chișinău?

Chișinău is the capital and the largest city of Moldova. It’s the political, economic, and cultural core of the country. First mentioned in 1436, it became the capital in 1812. Today, the city combines historical roots with green parks and modern development.

A City Full of Life

Chișinău is home to vibrant theaters, live music, museums, and public art. You’ll find cozy cafés, open-air markets, and festivals throughout the year. The city is welcoming both during the day and after sunset.

Easy to Get Around

Buses, trolleybuses, and minibuses operate all across the city. The international airport offers flights to many major cities in Europe. Chișinău is compact, walkable, and accessible.
